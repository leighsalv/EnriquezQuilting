About EMQ

At Enriquez Materials & Quilting, Inc., we show our customers how much we care. We use our years of experience to keep you informed of our ever-changing industry. For 15 years, we have earned valued relationships with customers like you through offering a complete product line, responding courteously to your needs, and providing the best value at competitive prices.

We are committed to:

  • Best value to our customers
